Chicago-based rapper Chance the Rapper has been in the music scene ever since he released his first mixtape in 2011, 10 Days, which he recorded after being suspended from his high school for, you guessed it, 10 days, for possession of marijuana. He has since released two other mixtapes, Acid Rap and Coloring Book. What makes him stand out among other rappers is that he is not signed to any record label, instead choosing to continue being an independent rapper. What really makes him stand out to me is his voice. I listen to him every morning to get me ready for the rest of the day.
